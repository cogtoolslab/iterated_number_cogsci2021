Welcome!

In this HIT, you will see a set of hand-drawn symbols, and will be asked what each one means. Before starting the task, you will be given further instructions and must pass a short quiz testing your understanding of the instructions.

Please note that if you have recently completed a HIT from this requester (either LadLab, or CogToolsLab), you might be unable to participate in this HIT. If that happens, you may receive a browser pop-up asking if you want to leave (please click 'leave'). We apologize in advance, and thank you for your interest!

We expect the average HIT to last approximately 10 minutes, including the time it takes to read instructions.

Note: We recommend using Chrome. We have not tested this HIT in other browsers.
